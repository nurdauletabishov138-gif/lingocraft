Eating Out!

An Interactive Worksheet for Developing Linguacreative Competence
🎯 Goal of the lesson: to play with English words, invent new ones, and express your own ideas about restaurants and eating out in a creative way!

💡 Activity 3 — Invent Your Own Food Idiom

An idiom is a colorful expression whose meaning isn't literal. Invent your OWN idiom connected to food or restaurants, then explain it.

💬 Activity 4 — Comic Strip — Fill the Bubbles

Choose funny or surprising dialogue for each scene. Be as creative as you can — invent reactions, jokes, or new expressions!

The waiter just brought you a plate you definitely didn't order.
